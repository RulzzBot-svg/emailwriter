ProDraft Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What ProDraft does

ProDraft helps you rewrite email drafts. The browser extension reads text from your compose box only when you open ProDraft and click Generate. ProDraft does not send emails on your behalf.

What we send to our servers

  • Email draft text you choose to polish
  • Tone and length preferences
  • An anonymous device ID for usage limits and billing

Third-party AI processing

Draft text is sent to our backend, which calls Google Gemini to generate a polished version. Google’s terms and privacy policy apply to that processing.

What we do not do

  • We do not read your inbox or email list automatically
  • We do not store your emails long-term unless required for abuse prevention
  • We do not sell your data

Contact

Questions about privacy? Use the suggestion form on the ProDraft website or contact the developer email listed on the Chrome Web Store listing.
